Welcome to university

Welcome to the Faculty of Humanities at the University of Debrecen. The Faculty of Humanities at the University of Debrecen is home to a great variety of institutes and departments all devoted to excellence in education and research. Our students may learn about the linguistic, literary, historical and cultural heritage of universal human culture and also acquire skills that enable them to pass this knowledge on to future generations.

Robert KEMENYFI
Dean, Arts
